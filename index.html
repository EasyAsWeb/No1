Kiwisaver

KiwiSaver is a voluntary, work-based savings initiative to help you with your long-term saving for retirement. It's designed to be hassle-free so it's easy to maintain a regular savings pattern.

Like it or not, KiwiSaver is here to stay and you're going to have to learn to use it well if you want to reap the rewards it offers. As one of the most regulated investment products in the country, with government contributions and dollar for dollar matching by employers, KiwiSaver is one of the most exciting assets you can own.
